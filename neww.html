Have Questions? We Have Answers.

For Parents

How do you ensure my child's safety?

Every profile undergoes manual verification of ID and contact info. You also have full privacy controls over photos and contact details, ensuring they are only shared with your approval.

Can I manage the profile myself?

Yes, absolutely. Our platform is designed for family involvement. You can create and manage the profile, communicate with our team, and review matches together with your son or daughter.

How does WhatsApp matching work?

Once we find a highly compatible match, we send the profile details directly to your registered WhatsApp number. This makes it easy and private to share and discuss with your family members.

For Individuals

Is this platform for divorced individuals?

Yes. We warmly welcome individuals from all life journeys—single, divorced, or widowed. We believe everyone deserves a chance at happiness and provide a respectful community to find a partner.

What does the 'Lifetime Plan' really mean?

It means you pay a single, one-time fee. Your profile remains active with full benefits until you find your partner and ask us to deactivate it. There are absolutely no recurring charges or hidden fees.

How fast is customer support?

We pride ourselves on our accessibility. Our support team is available via phone and WhatsApp during business hours to provide prompt, real-human assistance for any questions or support you may need.
